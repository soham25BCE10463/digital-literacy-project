# College Student Social Media Checklist





#### 5 Do's: 



Do regularly review and update your privacy settings across all personal accounts.



Do use LinkedIn actively to showcase your hackathon wins, projects, and academic milestones.



Do assume everything you post online is permanent and public, even on private stories.



Do use a professional tone and proper grammar when messaging university staff or alumni on any platform.



Do fact-check information and articles before sharing them to avoid spreading misinformation.





#### 5 Don'ts: 



Don't post emotional or negative rants about professors, peers, or university policies.



Don't share sensitive personal information like your exact hostel room number, phone number, or schedule publicly.



Don't use inappropriate, offensive, or discriminatory language in comments or posts.



Don't mix your professional and personal digital footprints (e.g., using an unprofessional handle for GitHub or Kaggle).



Don't engage in or encourage cyberbullying or harassment in group chats or comment sections.
